Internship opportunity

When you intern at AnkurGECA Incubation Centre Foundation (AICF), you will have a chance to work on vital assignments and initiatives, gaining hands-on experience that will help you grow both personally and professionally. As part of the internship program, you will apply your skillset in a supportive and collaborative environment while contributing to activities that make measurable real-world impact during your internship experience.

There are plenty of opportunities to do just not at AICF. Here, you will apply your skills in a supportive and collaborative environment while interacting with various academic institutions, corporate bodies/association business incubators etc.
